Privacy Policy

Rialtic MCP – Internal Tool  |  Effective: April 29, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Rialtic MCP QuickBooks integration ("the Tool") handles data. This is an internal tool used exclusively by authorized Rialtic employees and is not available to the general public.

2. Data We Access

The Tool accesses QuickBooks Online data via Intuit's OAuth 2.0 API, including financial records, company information, invoices, bills, and related accounting data, solely as needed for internal business operations at Rialtic.

3. How Data Is Used

Data accessed through the Tool is used exclusively for Rialtic's internal financial management and reporting purposes. Data is not sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties.

4. Data Storage

The Tool does not persistently store QuickBooks data outside of Intuit's authorized systems. OAuth tokens are stored locally on the authorized user's machine and are not transmitted to external servers.

5. Data Security

Access is restricted to authorized Rialtic personnel. OAuth credentials follow Intuit's security standards. Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their local credentials.

6. Your Rights

Authorized users may request revocation of access at any time by contacting Rialtic's Finance or IT team. Revoking access will disable the Tool's ability to access QuickBooks data.

7. Changes to This Policy

Rialtic may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Continued use of the Tool constitutes acceptance of any updates.

8. Contact

Privacy-related questions may be directed to matt.hopper@rialtic.io.
